4Recruiters FAQ

I receive a significant amount of emails, messages, smoke signals and otherwise about various opportunities. I do my best to be curious and provide responses where appreciate but there's only so much time in the day. Here are a list of common questions and answers which might help filter/limit waste in communication. These aren’t the only/most important things I’m looking for/value however they are the easiest to figure out before having more conversations.

My common Questions

1. What is the culture of the company? (I’m looking for concrete examples for the most part, not “They have a great culture”. Every company has a great culture for certain individuals but not for everyone.) If you don’t know that’s ok as well, just let me know.

2. What is the salary range? (It doesn’t need to be specific and is obviously dependent on conversations but having the low and high band at least can help facilitate if it’s a good fit)

3. If the salary range is influenced by equity, what does it typically look like? (ie. What’s the current valuation range? What are the vesting schedules, etc.)

4. What is the work structure and flexibility of the group? (ie. Is the communication structured for partial/full remote situations, is being physically visible more prioritized over deliveries?)

My common Answers

1. I currently travel all over the world, work remote, on-site in various countries when needed or embedded in groups. I highly value flexibility therefore if the groups doesn’t have that, it will need to be made up in the other parts of the group. (Exceptional compensation, group culture, technology, etc)

2. I’m open to C/Director level positions. However, I’m not well suited for manager positions unless it’s trying to change the structure of the group to be self directed.

3. I’ve worked in many other areas that aren’t listed in my linked-in. Feel free to ask me about them

4. I’m willing to give intros into my network for possible individuals that are a good fit for the opportunity - Please look for specific individuals in my network

5. If you ask me about providing a resume, I'll usually refer you to my linked-in. You can easily export it as a PDF if you need a physical copy. Ask me if you don't know how ;)

6. I can legally work in the US and in the EU without a visa
